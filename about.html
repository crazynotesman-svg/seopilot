About SEOPilot

We believe that SEO shouldn't cost hundreds of dollars per month. SEOPilot was built to give every content creator, blogger, and small business owner access to professional-grade SEO tools — completely free.

Our Mission

The SEO tool market is dominated by enterprise products like Semrush and Ahrefs, with price tags starting at $100+/month. That's simply out of reach for most independent creators who are just getting started. Our mission is to democratize SEO by providing free, high-quality tools that anyone can use without creating an account or reaching for a credit card.

What Makes Us Different

  • 100% Free Core Tools — No trial, no credit card, no "freemium" bait-and-switch.
  • No Sign-Up Required — Use every tool instantly. We don't even have a registration form.
  • Privacy First — Your content stays in your browser. We don't store, track, or sell your data.
  • AI-Powered — Where AI adds value (like title generation), we integrate it thoughtfully.
  • Education-Focused — Every tool includes guides explaining the "why" behind the metrics.

The Team

SEOPilot is an independent project built by a small team passionate about making the web more accessible. We're SEO practitioners ourselves who got tired of paying enterprise prices for tools we only used a fraction of. This is our answer.

Our Technology

SEOPilot runs on a modern, lightweight stack designed for speed and reliability. All tools are built with vanilla JavaScript and run directly in your browser. The site is hosted on Cloudflare's global edge network, ensuring fast load times from anywhere in the world. We use privacy-friendly analytics that don't track individual users.

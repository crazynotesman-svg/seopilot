Why Great Titles Matter for SEO

Your title is the first thing searchers see in Google results. Research shows that 80% of people read headlines, but only 20% read the rest. A well-crafted title can double your click-through rate (CTR), which is a positive ranking signal for Google. Our AI generates titles that balance SEO best practices with human psychology — including your keyword naturally while creating curiosity and urgency.

Title Formulas That Work

  • How-to — "How to [Achieve Desired Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
  • Listicle — "[Number] [Adjective] Ways to [Solve Problem]"
  • Comparison — "[Option A] vs [Option B]: Which Is Better for [Use Case]?"
  • Guide — "The Ultimate Guide to [Topic] for [Audience]"
  • Mistake-Focused — "[Number] Common [Topic] Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)"

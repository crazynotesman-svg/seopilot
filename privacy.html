Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At SEOPilot, we take your privacy seriously. Our core principle is simple: we don't want your data. We've designed our tools to work entirely in your browser, meaning your content never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to use an AI-powered feature that requires an API call.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous usage data through Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies or track individual users. Specifically:

  • No personal information — We don't ask for your name, email, or any identifying details.
  • No cookies — We don't set any tracking cookies on your browser.
  • No user accounts — There is no registration system on our site.
  • Anonymous page views — We see aggregate data like "how many people visited the SERP Preview tool today."

3. Tool Data Processing

Most of our tools process your input entirely within your browser. For tools that use AI APIs (such as the AI Title Generator), your input keywords are sent to our AI provider's API for processing. We do not store, log, or retain any of this data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — Hosting and CDN. Cloudflare processes visitor IP addresses for security and performance. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • AI API Providers — When you use AI-powered tools, your input is sent to the AI model API for processing. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers.
  • Google Fonts — We load fonts from Google Fonts for consistent typography. Google Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising (Future)

Currently, SEOPilot does not display any advertisements. If we introduce advertising in the future (such as Google AdSense), we will update this policy and provide clear notice. Any advertising will comply with applicable privacy regulations.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
